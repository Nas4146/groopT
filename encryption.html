üîß How It Works

Messages: When you send a message in GroopTroop:

  1. Your message is encrypted on your device before leaving your phone
  2. Only your group members have the keys to decrypt the message
  3. Even GroopTroop servers cannot read your encrypted messages
  4. Messages remain encrypted during transmission and storage

Photos & Media: When you share images in GroopTroop:

  1. Photos are encrypted using the same algorithm as your messages
  2. Encrypted image data is uploaded to secure cloud storage
  3. Group members automatically decrypt images when viewing
  4. Decrypted photos are cached locally for instant loading
  5. Original unencrypted photos never leave your device

  • Random Generation: Cryptographically secure random number generators
  • Media Cache: Encrypted photos cached in app-private directories
